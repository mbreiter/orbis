Welcome!¶

Welcome to the 2016 Orbis Challenge! This year’s Orbis Challenge will involve creating an AI for Cyber Team Zero, where 2 squads of robot super-soldiers use cunning tactics to capture control points and eliminate the enemy squad.

Note

Even if you are familiar with Java or Python, you should still read the documentation. The documents contain important information about the game, grading and competition rules.
